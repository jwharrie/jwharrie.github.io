Who I Am

I'm currently a Vancouver-based software developer for LinkPoint Technology Group where I develop interesting and exciting software projects for business clients across BC. I graduated from Simon Fraser University with a Computer Science degree and have co-op experience as a Test Developer for MEDFAR Clinical Solutions PLEXIA, a medical software company that develops web and mobile applications for over 1000 BC and YK physicians.

In my roles I thrived using my great teamwork and collaboration skills, excellent communication and interpersonal abilities, strong problem-solving, high perseverance and a deep willingness to learn and grow as a professional.

Outside of work I like to develop personal software projects and catch up on the latest tech developments through blogs and articles. I like to bring my ideas to life using Python, Java, C++ and JavaScript. I express myself with both web and mobile apps.

Besides tech, I like to read books, teach myself Japanese and follow the latest developments in blockchain tech.

As a commited tech worker with lifelong learning aspirations I'm always open for opportunities in software development. I'm open to relocate where my passion takes me, so don't be shy to reach out to me if your interested in connecting!
